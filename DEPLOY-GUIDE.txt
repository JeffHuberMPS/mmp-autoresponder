==========================================================
  GET YOUR AUTORESPONDER RUNNING 24/7 (laptop off)  — Render
==========================================================

Everything is already built and tested in this folder. To put it online so it
replies even when your computer is off, we host it for FREE on Render.
No credit card. Claude will drive the technical parts — you just create the
two free accounts and click a few buttons.

WHAT YOU GET: a permanent web address like
   https://mmp-autoresponder.onrender.com
that Instagram talks to 24/7.

--------------------------------------------------------
THE STEPS (Claude does the gray parts WITH you)
--------------------------------------------------------

1. FREE GITHUB ACCOUNT  (this is where your code lives)
   - Go to github.com  →  Sign up  (email + password, free)
   - (Skip if you already have one — just log in.)

2. PUT THE CODE ON GITHUB
   - Claude creates a new repo and pushes this folder up for you.
   - (You may get one browser pop-up to authorize the push — click Authorize.)

3. FREE RENDER ACCOUNT  (the host)
   - Go to render.com  →  Get Started  →  "Sign in with GitHub"
   - Authorize Render to see your repos. (Free, no card.)

4. DEPLOY
   - In Render:  New +  →  Blueprint  →  pick the mmp-autoresponder repo
   - Render reads render.yaml and sets it up automatically.

5. PASTE 3 SECRETS  (Claude gives you the exact values)
   In Render's Environment settings, paste:
   - ANTHROPIC_API_KEY  (your Claude key)
   - IG_ACCESS_TOKEN    (your Instagram token)
   - IG_APP_SECRET      (your Instagram app secret)
   (IG_VERIFY_TOKEN and IG_GRAPH_BASE are already filled in.)

6. CLICK DEPLOY  →  wait ~2 min  →  you get your permanent URL.

7. POINT INSTAGRAM AT IT  (Claude does this)
   In your Meta app webhook settings, change the Callback URL to:
       https://YOUR-RENDER-URL/webhook/instagram
   Verify token stays:  jarvis-secret-handshake
   → Verify and save.  Done. It now runs 24/7.
